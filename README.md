# EDA-Superstore-Sales

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the Kaggle Superstore Sales dataset, focusing on three areas: 
product profitability, regional performance, and customer segmentation.

## Key Findings

1. **Product Analysis**: Technology generates the highest profit at $145,454 — 
   7.8x more than Furniture. It is recommended to reduce investment in Furniture 
   and reallocate resources toward Technology and Office Supplies.

2. **Regional Analysis**: The West region leads in both total sales ($725,457) 
   and profit margin (14.9%). However, the Central region has the lowest profit 
   margin at 7.9%, suggesting a possible price war strategy to capture market share.

3. **Customer Analysis**: The majority of customers fall into the average buyer segment. 
   VIP services are recommended for customers with total spending exceeding $10,000, 
   while regular promotions should be used to improve retention among general customers.
